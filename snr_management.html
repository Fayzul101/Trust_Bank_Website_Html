Chairman

Ahsan Zaman Chowdhury

Mr. Ahsan Zaman Chowdhury, a seasoned banking professional with nearly four decades of experience, has held key roles at AB Bank, Eastern Bank Limited, and Trust Bank PLC. Joining Trust Bank in 2016, he rose to Deputy Managing Director and Chief Business Officer in 2020 and later served as Managing Director (In-Charge). Renowned for his expertise in business development, corporate banking, and strategic decision-making, he has driven profitability and excellence at Trust Bank. A graduate of the University of Chittagong, he is also an avid golfer, traveler, and active member of socio-cultural organizations, embodying leadership and dedication in all facets of his life.
